Web Performance Working Group

Our Mission

We provide methods to observe and improve aspects of application performance of user agent features and APIs.

The Co-Chairs of the Working Group are Ilya Grigorik, Todd Reifsteck and Yoav Weiss. The W3C Team Contacts for the Web Performance Working Group are Philippe Le Hégaret and Xiaoqian Wu.

What is going on?

We meet bi-weekly to explore new Performance APIs, and discuss open issue of the current APIs. Visit our Teleconference Page or subscribe to our mailing list if you are interested. Meeting notes and announcements are also available in the group's official Twitter account @webperfwg.
